Emergency Helpline Numbers in India

Ambulance
Medical emergency and ambulance services.
Phone: 102
Email: health@india.gov.in

Fire Service
Fire and rescue emergency services.
Phone: 101
Email: fire@india.gov.in

Women Helpline
Support and safety services for women.
Phone: 1091
Email: women@india.gov.in

Child Helpline
Assistance for children in distress.
Phone: 1098
Email: child@india.gov.in

Disaster Management
Help during natural disasters and emergencies.
Phone: 108
Email: disaster@india.gov.in
